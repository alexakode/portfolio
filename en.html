About Me

During the frontend course Kodehode, I have expanded my skills and learned to develop solutions using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I am particularly focused on accessibility, both linguistic and visual, utilising colours that adapt to users and systems.

With a background in language studies and translation, I have acquired an understanding of structure, adaptation, and localisation. I have experience translating free software on multiple platforms, including Transifex and Launchpad.

In my opinion, good frontend development means meeting users where they are, whether it concerns language, screen, or preferences.
